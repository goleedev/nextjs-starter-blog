### [XD Guru](https://www.xdguru.com/)

![xdguru](xdguru.png)

#### How To

1) Search '3D' to find 3d resources

2) Download free xd files

3) Open files on Adobe XD (use 1mo. free trial)

4) Click and drag to copy SVG codes for the resource
