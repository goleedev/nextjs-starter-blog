### Quotes in Strings

### 1) Mixing with double and single quotes

```js
console.log("Hi, 'there!'");
or
console.log('Hi, "there!"');
```
---
### 2) Use Backslash Instead

```js
console.log('We\'re here.');
or
console.log("She said, \"That is correct.\"");
```
